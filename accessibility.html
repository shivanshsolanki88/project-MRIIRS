Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: May 24, 2025

Our Commitment

The Haryana Sarkar Yojana Portal is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

Accessibility Features

We have implemented the following accessibility features:

  • Keyboard Navigation: All functionality is available using a keyboard.
  • Screen Reader Compatibility: The portal is compatible with popular screen readers.
  • Text Resizing: Text can be resized using browser zoom functions.
  • Color Contrast: Sufficient color contrast for text and background elements.
  • Alternative Text: Images include descriptive alt text.
  • Clear Structure: Proper heading structure and logical reading order.

Conformance Status

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards. Our portal is partially conformant, meaning some content may not fully conform to the accessibility standard.

Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of the Haryana Sarkar Yojana Portal. Please let us know if you encounter accessibility barriers:

  • Email: accessibility@haryanayojana.gov.in
  • Phone: 1800-180-2025 (Accessibility Helpline)
  • Postal Address: Haryana Secretariat, Chandigarh

We try to respond to feedback within 5 business days.

Technical Specifications

Accessibility of this portal relies on the following technologies:

  • HTML
  • WAI-ARIA
  • CSS
  • JavaScript

Assessment Approach

The accessibility of this portal was assessed by our internal team using manual testing and automated tools. We also conducted user testing with individuals with disabilities.

Continuous Improvement

We are committed to regularly reviewing and improving the accessibility of our portal. Our development process includes accessibility considerations at all stages.
