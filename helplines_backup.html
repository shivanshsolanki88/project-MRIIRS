Stay Safe: Fraud & Scam Awareness
  • Never share OTPs, PINs, or passwords with anyone. Banks or officials will never ask for them.
  • Do not click on suspicious links in SMS or WhatsApp messages promising rewards or warning of penalties.
  • Beware of "Digital Arrest" or blackmail scams where callers impersonate police or government officials.
  • If you suspect a scam, report it immediately by calling 1930 or visiting cybercrime.gov.in.
